# UL Lafayette — Research Centers, Laboratories & Faculty Research

> UL Lafayette is a Carnegie R1 (Doctoral University — Very High Research Activity) institution. The NSF ranks it among the top 10 U.S. universities for the percentage of R&D funded by business. The university is also one of 250 institutions worldwide in the **National Academy of Inventors**.
