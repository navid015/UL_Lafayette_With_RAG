## Program-Specific Highlights

- **Nursing:** 95% NCLEX pass rate for 38 consecutive years
- **Online Programs:** Highest ranked online bachelor's programs in Louisiana (2026 U.S. News)
- **Engineering:** Top-10 electrical engineering graduate program nationally
- **Animation:** #14 in the South, #37 nationally among public schools
- **MBA:** Top-tier North American MBA program (CEO Magazine)
- **Computer Science:** Ph.D. program since 1969 — one of the earliest in the nation
- **Francophone Studies:** One of only three doctoral programs in Francophone studies in the world
- **Industrial Design:** Louisiana's only industrial design degree
- **Informatics:** Louisiana's only master's in informatics
