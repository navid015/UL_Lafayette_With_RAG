## About the President — Dr. Ramesh Kolluru

Dr. Ramesh Kolluru serves as the President of the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. He is also a Professor of Computer Science and a founding figure in disaster management research. He served as the first Executive Director of the NIMSAT Institute (2008), which works on homeland security and emergency management. His research interests include Control Systems, Health Informatics, and Social Computing.
