### Civil Engineering Research Areas
- **Water Resources Engineering:** Flood modeling, watershed hydrology, climate adaptation, coastal erosion, shore protection
- **Environmental Engineering:** Wastewater treatment, soil contamination remediation, waste-to-energy conversion, NASA Mars environmental management
- **Structural Engineering:** High-rise buildings, bridges, cranes, structural materials, aging infrastructure inspection
- **Transportation Engineering:** Pavement design, materials, intelligent transportation systems, traffic safety
- **Civil Infrastructure Materials:** Pavement composites, green construction materials (rice/sugarcane byproducts), nanotechnology in infrastructure
