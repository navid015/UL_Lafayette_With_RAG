**College of Education & Human Development:**

**University College:**
- Graduate Certificates (various)

### Graduate Funding & Support
- Graduate assistantships (teaching and research) available across departments
- Graduate fellowships offered by individual departments
- TOPS does not apply to graduate students, but other financial aid available

### Admissions Requirements
- General Graduate School admission requirements apply to all programs
- Individual programs may have additional requirements
- GRE required/optional varies by program
- International students: TOEFL/IELTS required
